Dialysis Care Guide

Essential information for patients and caregivers

Equipment & Supplies

  • Dialyzer: The artificial kidney filter
  • Bloodlines: Tubing for blood transport
  • Fistula Needles: Specialized access needles
  • Cycler: Machine for peritoneal dialysis

Access Care

  • Fistula/Graft: Permanent surgical access
  • Protective Clothing: Hoodies with zippers
  • Care: Antibacterial soap & antiseptic
  • Comfort: Numbing sprays & creams

Diet & Meds

  • Binders: Manage phosphorus levels
  • EPO: Hormone for anemia management
  • Supplements: Iron, B-complex & Vit C
  • Low-Sodium: Kidney-friendly spices

Key Terms

  • Dry Weight: Post-session target weight
  • Exchange: PD draining/filling process
  • Kt/V: Measure of dialysis adequacy
  • Access: Site for blood removal
